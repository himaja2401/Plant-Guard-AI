About PlantGuard AI

Learn about our mission, team, and the technology behind our plant disease detection system.

Revolutionizing Plant Health Management

PlantGuard AI was founded in 2023 with a simple yet powerful mission: to make advanced plant disease detection accessible to everyone, from home gardeners to commercial farmers.

Our platform combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with agricultural expertise to provide instant, accurate diagnoses of plant diseases through simple image uploads.

With over 25 plant diseases in our database and a 96.7% accuracy rate, we're helping growers around the world protect their crops and improve yields.

Our Mission

To democratize plant health management by providing accessible, accurate, and instant disease detection technology that empowers growers of all scales to protect their crops and contribute to global food security.
